UX Studio

Worked with three other students at Rocheste Institute of Technology in order to create a high-fidelity wireframe prototype for a local nonprofit. We chose the Catholic Family Center, and wireframed an app for Crisis Intervention. We began by choosing a color scheme that matched the website and foundation's aesthetic. We then began researching to see what other crsis intervention apps were structured like and started getting a stronger idea of what was important. This helped us to develop some bare bones lo-fi wireframes. We quickly switched over to develop a hi-fi prototype which we then presented to a panel of judges and all other participants. Check out the wireframes on Figma.
